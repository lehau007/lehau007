Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 11, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how our Facebook Messenger chatbot ("the Bot") collects, uses, and shares information when you interact with it.

2. Information We Collect

When you use our Bot, we may collect:

  • Messages: The text messages you send to our Bot
  • Facebook User ID: Your unique Facebook identifier (not your name or profile information)
  • Interaction Data: Information about how you interact with the Bot

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide responses to your messages
  • Improve the Bot's functionality and user experience
  • Search the web for information when requested
  • Maintain and troubleshoot the Bot

4. Information Sharing

We do not:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your information with advertisers
  • Store your conversation history permanently

We may share information with:

  • AI Service Providers: Your messages are sent to AI language models (OpenAI) to generate responses
  • Search Services: Search queries may be sent to web search APIs (DuckDuckGo) when you request information

5. Data Retention

We do not store conversation history. Messages are processed in real-time and are not permanently stored on our servers.

6. Third-Party Services

Our Bot uses the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI/NVIDIA AI: For generating chatbot responses
  • DuckDuckGo: For web search functionality
  • Facebook Messenger Platform: For message delivery

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of information.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Stop using the Bot at any time
  • Delete your conversation by blocking the Bot on Facebook
  • Request information about data we collect

8. Children's Privacy

Our Bot is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates when the policy was last revised.
